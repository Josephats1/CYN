Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to Hope Foundation?

You can donate online through our secure donation portal, by phone at (555) 123-4567, or by mailing a check to our office address. We also accept stock donations and planned giving options.

What volunteer opportunities are available?

We have various volunteer opportunities including tutoring, event support, administrative help, and program-specific roles. Visit our Volunteer page or contact our Volunteer Coordinator for current opportunities.

How can my company partner with Hope Foundation?

We offer corporate partnership opportunities including sponsorships, employee engagement programs, and cause-related marketing. Contact our Development Director to discuss partnership options.

How much of my donation goes directly to programs?

85% of every dollar donated goes directly to program services. 10% supports fundraising efforts, and 5% covers administrative costs. We're proud of our efficient use of donor funds.
